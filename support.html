Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?
Yes. All session data is stored on your device and syncs privately via iCloud. PokerTrackPro never sends your data to any external server.
How do I log a past session?
Tap the + button, fill in your session details, and enable the Manual Entry toggle. You can set a custom start and end time along with your cash-out amount.
How do I enable Bankroll Tracking?
Go to Settings and toggle on "Enable Bankroll Tracking." A Bankroll tab will appear where you can log deposits, withdrawals, and view your current balance.
How does the Apple Watch companion work?
Open a live session on your iPhone, then launch the PokerTrackPro app on your Apple Watch. It will show your running timer and current profit/loss in real time.
Can I export my sessions?
Yes. Go to Settings → Export Sessions to download a CSV of your full session history, which you can open in Excel, Numbers, or any spreadsheet app.
Does PokerTrackPro facilitate gambling or real-money play?
No. PokerTrackPro is a personal session journal — like a fitness tracker for your poker game. It does not facilitate gambling, wagering, or real-money play of any kind.
